By enabling this clause, the following declaration will be included in your will:

"Should I no longer be capable of making decisions for my own future and should my physical and/or mental condition deteriorate to such an extent that there is no reasonable prospect of my recovery, I request that I be allowed to die and that no life support systems be used to keep me alive.

Living Will Clause i A living will declares your wishes regarding life-sustaining medical treatment should you become incapacitated. It is included as a clause in this will. Include a living will declaration in this will?

By enabling this clause, the following declaration will be included in your will:

"Should I no longer be capable of making decisions for my own future and should my physical and/or mental condition deteriorate to such an extent that there is no reasonable prospect of my recovery, I request that I be allowed to die and that no life support systems be used to keep me alive. I further request that I be given whatever quantity of drugs may be required to keep me free from pain or distress even if the moment of death is hastened."
